Jo Constantz

Jo Constantz is a skilled reporter who is passionate about rigorous research and investigative journalism, with a focus on business, labor, and the economy. Jo gained valuable reporting and fact-checking experience through stints at the New York Times, the Intercept, and Los Angeles-based Capital and Main. A master's candidate at The Craig Newmark Graduate School of Journalism, she was selected for a full merit-based scholarship funded by the Sulzberger foundation and has built an advanced data analysis skillset in Excel and Python. She studied Political & Social Thought at the University of Virginia, graduating Phi Beta Kappa with highest honors.
