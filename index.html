Jo Constantz

Jo is an investigative journalist based in Brooklyn, pursuing a master's degree at the Craig Newmark Graduate School of Journalism at CUNY. She's studying business and economics reporting and building an advanced skillset in audio production and data analysis. She served as a News Assistant at the New York Times and has contributed research and fact-checking to a number of organizations, including the Intercept, Topic Magazine, Rewire News Group, and n+1.
